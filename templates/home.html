Mission

Vision

Creating a sustainable environment where humans and wildlife thrive together in harmony, free from conflict and mutual threats.

The Crises

Raid on Crop Farms

Wild animals, particularly elephants, often raid farms in search of food, leading to massive crop destruction. This not only threatens food security but also results in significant financial losses for farmers who rely on agriculture for their livelihood.

Loss of an Endangered Species

The increasing conflict between humans and wildlife has also led to the tragic loss of endangered species. Lions, among other threatened animals, often fall victim to retaliatory killings or habitat destruction. These contribute to the decline of already vulnerable populations, pushing some species closer to extinction.

Destruction of Property

Large animals like elephants often trample homes and destroy infrastructure, displacing families in search of food and causing significant economic and emotional distress in affected communities.
